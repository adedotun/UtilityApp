No! All processing happens directly in your browser. Your files never leave your device, ensuring complete privacy and security.

Yes! The "Sign PDF" tool lets you draw signatures, add text, and insert dates. You can save signatures for reuse and sign multiple pages.

Is there a file size limit?

There's no hard limit, but very large files may take longer to process and depend on your device's available memory. Most standard documents work perfectly.
